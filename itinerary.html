The 3-Day Blueprint

Planning a trip to Dublin for a weekend? We’ve got you covered. This 3-day itinerary is designed to help you experience the very best of the city without feeling rushed or overwhelmed. Whether you're here for culture, food, or a bit of craic, this guide brings everything together in a simple, enjoyable way.


And if you're lucky… you might even get a sunny weekend (we can dream, right? 😄)

Day 1 — Dublin City Highlights

Morning

Start your Dublin adventure in the heart of the city with breakfast at Beanhive Coffee on Dawson Street. Then visit Trinity College and the Book of Kells, and stop by the Molly Malone Statue.

Afternoon

Visit Dublin Castle, walk down Grafton Street, and grab a spice bag from Xian Street Food. Enjoy it in St. Stephen’s Green — just watch out for the seagulls!

Evening

Visit the National Museum, then head to Sophie’s Rooftop for dinner. End the night in Temple Bar with live music at Merchant’s Arch.

Day 2 — Coastal Escape to Howth

Morning

Take the DART to Howth and explore the market for breakfast. Pick up snacks for a picnic before heading out.

Midday

Walk the Howth Cliff Loop (around 2 hours) with incredible coastal views and lighthouse scenery.

Afternoon & Evening

Visit the hidden beach below the cliffs — steep but worth it. Finish with seafood at Howth Harbour before heading back to Dublin for a pint.

Day 3 — Culture & History

Morning

Start at Bread 41 for coffee and pastries.

Midday

Visit Jameson Distillery and the Guinness Storehouse for a fun and interactive experience.

Afternoon & Evening

Explore Kilmainham Gaol, then finish your trip with dinner at The Church, with live Irish music and dancing.

Final Thoughts

Three days in Dublin is the perfect amount of time to experience the city’s highlights, enjoy amazing food, and take in unforgettable views.
