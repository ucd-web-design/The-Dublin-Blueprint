Top Attractions

Discover Dublin’s most iconic landmarks, from historic cathedrals and cultural institutions to lively city streets. Whether you're drawn to history, architecture, or simply soaking up the atmosphere, these are the places that truly capture the spirit of the city.

Dublinia

An interactive museum that brings Dublin’s Viking and medieval history to life.

Location: Dublin 8

Price: €18 / €11.50

St Patrick’s Cathedral

Ireland’s largest cathedral, dating back to 1220 with stunning architecture.

Location: Dublin

Price: €11.50 / €5.50

Trinity College & Book of Kells

Home to the 9th-century Book of Kells and the Long Room Library.

Location: Trinity College Dublin

Price: €25.50 / €13.50

The Spire

A 120m stainless steel landmark on O’Connell Street.

Location: O’Connell Street

Price: Free

GPO

Historic site of the 1916 Rising with immersive exhibitions.

Location: O’Connell Street

Price: Free / €15

Guinness Storehouse

Explore Ireland’s most famous beer and enjoy views from the Gravity Bar.

Location: Dublin 8

Price: €20–€30

Temple Bar

Dublin’s most famous cultural quarter with pubs and live music.

Location: Dublin 2

Price: Free

National Museum of Ireland

Discover Irish history, artefacts, and ancient treasures.

Location: Dublin 2

Price: Free
