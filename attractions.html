Top Attractions

The must-sees, plus a few that make Dublin feel special.

Dublin is compact and easy to explore, which means you can see a lot without rushing. These are the places that give you the best feel for the city.

Trinity College & Book of Kells

Located in the heart of the city, Trinity College is one of Dublin’s most iconic landmarks. Its historic campus and the famous Book of Kells make it a must-visit.

Guinness Storehouse

One of Dublin’s most popular experiences, where you can learn the story of Guinness and enjoy panoramic views from the Gravity Bar.

Temple Bar

Known for its lively atmosphere, colourful streets and traditional pubs. A must for soaking up the city’s energy.

Hidden Gems

St Stephen’s Green

A peaceful escape right in the city centre.

George’s Street Arcade

A quirky indoor market with lots of character.

Howth Cliff Walk

Beautiful coastal views just outside Dublin.
