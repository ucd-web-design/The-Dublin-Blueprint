Welcome to The Dublin Blueprint

Planning your time in a new city can feel like a lot, especially when it’s your first time in Dublin. That’s exactly why this guide exists. We’ve brought together everything you need to explore the city in a way that feels simple, clear, and actually enjoyable. Whether you’re here for a few days or settling in for a semester, this guide will help you find your rhythm in Dublin - from the must-see spots to the places you’ll stumble upon and end up loving. Think of it as your starting point, your plan when you need one, and your guide when you don’t.
