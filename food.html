Food and Drink

The best Restaurants, Bars, and Cafes in Dublin City. To check out each spot's menu or to book a reservation, simply click on the name of your chosen spot to head straight to their website!

Fine Dining Casual Dining Bars and Pubs Cafes

Fine Dining

Chapter One

Located on Parnell Square, this is the pinnacle of Irish fine dining. Known for its impeccable service and the famous laminated treacle brioche.

Dax Restaurant

Consistently rated as one of the best for fine dining in Dublin, offering a sophisticated French-influenced menu in a Georgian basement.

Casual Eats & Trendy Spots

Howth Market

Fresh organic produce and antiques by the coast.

Bambino's

A NYC style slice shop selling Premium Slices & Squares. A must stop!

Xian Street Food

A cult favorite for Spice Bags and hand-pulled noodles in the city centre.

Grano

Authentic, handmade Calabrian pasta in Stoneybatter. Book well in advance!

Bars and Cafes

Vintage Cocktail Club

A hidden gem in Temple Bar. Ring the bell at the unmarked black door.

The Long Hall

Beautiful Victorian pub with arguably the best Guinness in Dublin.

Fidelity

An audiophile bar in Smithfield with a custom sound system and craft beer.

Cafes

Beanhive

Specialty coffee and yummy treats in the heart of the city.

Brother Hubbard

Vibrant Middle Eastern-inspired brunch institution.

Scéal Bakery

World-class sourdough and seasonal pastries on South Circular Road.
